About the Journal

Welcome to OpenResearchArchive, a student-run academic platform founded by passionate students in Pakistan. Our mission is to provide a space for young researchers, scholars, and innovators to share their work, ideas, and discoveries with the world. We publish high-quality papers across disciplines, encouraging critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration among students. By fostering a culture of inquiry and learning, OpenResearchArchive aims to amplify student voices, showcase emerging research, and inspire the next generation of scholars in Pakistan and beyond.
