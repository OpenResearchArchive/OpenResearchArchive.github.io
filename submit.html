Submit an Article

The Student Research Journal welcomes original research papers, review articles, and high-quality academic notes written by students. All submissions are reviewed by the editorial team before publication.

Who Can Submit?

Any student may submit an article, whether at O-Level, A-Level, undergraduate, or equivalent academic level.

Accepted Subjects

Physics, Economics, Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology, and other academic disciplines relevant to student research.

Formatting Requirements

Submissions should be in PDF format, clearly structured, and properly referenced. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.

Review Process

Each submission undergoes editorial review for clarity, originality, and academic quality before publication.
