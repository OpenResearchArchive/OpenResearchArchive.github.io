Submission Guidelines

  • Open to students worldwide
  • Original work only
  • PDF format
  • Proper citations required
  • Plagiarism not accepted
  • 1.Formatting Requirements:
  • 1.1 Font Style: The primary text must be in Times New Roman or EB Garamond. Footnotes and citations will follow the same font requirements.
  • 1.2 Font Size: The main body of the essay must be in 12 pt font, footnotes in 10 pt, and the essay title must be in 15 pt and bold.
  • 1.3 Spacing: The entire essay must have 1.5 line spacing, while block quotations and footnotes must be single-spaced.
  • 1.4 Text Style: The text must be fully justified, each paragraph must begin with a first-line indent of 0.5", and headings or subheadings may be used as long as they remain consistent throughout the essay.
  • 1.5 Page Layout: The essay must be typed on A4-sized pages with 1-inch margins on all sides, and page numbers must appear at the bottom center.
  • 2.Use of Sources
  • 2.1 Citation Rules: All statistics, quotations, historical claims, case studies, or policy references must be properly cited.
  • 2.2 Visual Data Requirements: Any graphs, charts, or data visuals included must contain a clear title, a source citation below the visual, and the year of data collection.
  • 2.3 Citation Style: Participants may use Harvard, APA, or Chicago (footnotes) citation styles but must remain consistent throughout the essay.
  • 2.4 Plagiarism Policy: Plagiarism exceeding 10% (excluding references) will result in automatic disqualification.
